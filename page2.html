WebLinguistika

Your free language analyzer! Helping you with your grammatical structure.


Weblinguistika provide on the spot feedback on grammar and spelling, it help users fast identify and learn for their mistake. This website is free to everyone, making it an inclusive resources to the learners from different backgrounds. A simple textbox with the "Done" button helps user to make it easy to interact with the tool. By underlining the incorrect words, the website actively promotes correct grammar. Using a familiar tool like auto-correct helps the bridge of the students who struggle in grammar, and allowing them to focus in improving their writing skills. Weblinguistika is a good website to anyone looking to improve their writing skills in English. Users are empowered to self-correct their text, which encourage independent learning and critical thinking about their own writing habits. By using the website, students can personalize the rules of grammar and spelling leading to improve writing ability. The positive increase of having no underline word on written sentences can boost their confidence and motivate them to keep practicing the English grammar. Teachers can use Weblinguistika as a additional tool in classroom, helping a student to writing and providing a way to monitoring progress in their language skill.
